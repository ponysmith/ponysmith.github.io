Autoplay Statuses

Slideshow status in shyft has one of four values:

start

The start status is a temporary status that occurs when the slideshow is being started. Once the slideshow is started, the status will automatically switch to play. This status is necessary so that a slideshow can be (re)started from the stop status.

stop

The stop status means that the slideshow has been completely stopped (not just paused).

play

The play status means that the slideshow is currently playing.

pause

The pause status means that the slideshow has been temporarily paused (but not stopped).
